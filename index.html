IJCNLP-AACL 2025 SLT4LRL Workshop

Sign Language Translation for Low-Resource Languages

Co-located with IJCNLP-AACL 2025 in Abu Dhabi, UAE on November 20th, 2025
Shared Task Important Dates

With over 430 million people worldwide experiencing disabling hearing loss, sign languages are vital for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing community. In India, with an estimated 63 million DHH individuals, Indian Sign Language (ISL) is a primary communication mode yet lacks sufficient technological support. This workshop aims to advance AI and NLP technologies for sign language processing, focusing on low-resource languages like ISL, to enhance accessibility in education, healthcare, and public services.

We invite researchers, students, and developers to contribute to our shared tasks and collaborate on building inclusive technologies for sign language users.
